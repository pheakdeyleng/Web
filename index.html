Rice paddy

Rice is a staple food in Cambodia. We eat it three times day for breakfast, lunch and dinner. When I bought my camera, I always enjoy taking landscape picture of the rice paddy. The rice paddy is beautiful in the rainy season.

River

There are alot of big rivers in Cambodia. The biggest one is Mekong River. It brings fish, fresh water and fertilizer to the land and the people.
